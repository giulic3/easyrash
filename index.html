easyrash

A simple and intuitive conference management system where you can read and review scientific papers written using RASH Framework, and partecipate in the most interesting events.

more about us

EasyRASH is based on RASH (Research Articles in Simplified HTML), a framework including a markup language defined as a subset of HTML+RDF for writing scientific articles.

EasyRASH makes use of the advantages provided by the framework and it offers to the users some unique experiences.

As a registered user, you can view and read all the papers submitted for an event in Reader mode, you can annotate the articles you were asked to review in Annotator mode and you can, as Chair, fulfill your duties evaluating a paper or a conference!
